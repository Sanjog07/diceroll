GamePlay

  • Roll dice to start the game.
  • If dice hits 1 then all the current score of the current player becomes 0 and now second player plays the game.
  • If any player clicks HOLD button then the total score of that player increases but now it's other player's turn to play the game.
  • Any player who hits 100 first wins the Game.
